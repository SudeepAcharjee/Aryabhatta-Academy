About

Aryabhatta Academy,An ideal english medium school (NCERT course curriculum) Under "Aryabhatta Trust".

2008 Established English Medium school

Introduction

Aryabhatta Academy an ideal English Medium School was established in the year 2008 at North Barpeta Road and run by "Aryabhatta Trust" with the view to impact an idealogy eduction to the students in NCERT course and curriculum. The school has been christened as ARYABHATTA ACADEMY keeping the aim and ideology of great Indian Mathematician Aryabhatta.

Our academy endevours to fullfill the hopes and inspiration of the children learning hailing from rural, urban and semi-urban area of the states. The academy is about 100 meters away from Barpeta Road Railway Station besdes Gobardhana Development Block Office.

Aims & Objective

The primary aim of our school is to apply the methods and techniques of teaching which are child-centric, developmnetally-appropriate and mostly based on play and activity. Our objective is the holistic developmnet of the child to enable her or him to realize her or his his maximum potential. To reach the goal,we adhere the following principles:

1. Our aim is to provide quality education at an affordable fee structure.

2. The academy aims to provide co-curricular activities like sports, arts & craft, yoga, music and dance and develop their self confidence.

3. Apart from textual leaming we aim to provide assignment, project, workshop, seminars to develop their knowledge technically by the experienced tacutes from different institutes and Universites.

4. We aim to provide career counseling frequently from the experts for the better career of the students.

5. We aim to prepare students to compete different National and International Level Technical competitions.

6. We aim to provide Computer Programming Language (Coding) from class VI onwards to Artificial Intelligence and Coding.

7. To develop the children scientifically and socially.

8. Play and activity as the basis of learning.

9 .Leaming without burden in a joyful atmosphere.

10. Transaction of Assamese as a subject of vital importance from Nursery class onwards.
