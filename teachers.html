From the Director's Desk

Abhijit Saha
M.Sc, M.tsch-IT
Ex-Lecturer NIT,Silchar & Nagaland University.

I offer my hearty welcome to all students and gurdians who have reposed their faith on ARYABHATTA ACADEMY.This institution is specifically dedicated to the pursuit of child-centric and activity-based approach The mission of this institution is to provide quality education. Currently, this institution is running from class Nursery to class X. H.S.LC under SEBA with a future plan for the opening of Higher Secondary at New campus. With a team of young, qualified, talented dedicated and experienced faculties, this academy is committed to impart a holistic environment for the all-round development of learners. In this regard, I sincerely solicit support and cooperation from one and all. With Thanks.

From the Principal's Desk

Dilip Kumar Saha

I have the privilege to welcome you all to the great temple of learning. I take the opportunity through this prospectus to thank one and everyone especially our parents who have unconditionally trusted us and wholeheartedly supported us. It has been running successfully since 2008. It has already seen innumerable growth and development in the areas of academic excellence and other co-curricular activities. Teaching and learning are at the heart of our efforts to make our academy more unified and more excellent in future. Our aim is to provide quality and standard education to each and every students. We constantly work to develop a good sense of discipline and sound moral character. I am delighted to see students getting involved in various activities that have been organised for them. Again I would like to express my deep sentiment of gratitude to everyone of you for your constant support, love and concern towards the academy. I am confident that Aryabhatta Academy has been serving as a guiding light to every student admitted to this institution. With Thanks.
